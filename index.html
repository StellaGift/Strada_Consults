Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

You got questions? Don't worry, we got answers. We try to compile list of questions we receive from prospects constantly and provide answers for them. If you did not see the questions on your mind on the list, feel free to reach out.

How do I buy or sell a property with your agency?

Our team guides you through every step, from property search or listing, negotiation, to closing, ensuring a smooth and secure transaction.

What software solutions can Strada build for my business?

We build custom web and mobile applications, secure backend systems, and scalable software tailored to your business goals and workflows.

How is property valuation determined?

We assess market trends, location, property condition, and comparable sales to provide accurate and reliable valuations.

Do you provide vulnerability assessment and penetration testing?

Yes. We perform structured assessments and authorized penetration tests to find vulnerabilities, validate risks, and provide prioritized remediation actions.

Do you manage rental properties?

Yes, we offer full property management services, including tenant sourcing, lease administration, maintenance, and financial reporting.

How do your cloud security and DevSecOps services work?

We secure cloud infrastructure with proactive controls, continuous monitoring, and automated security checks embedded into your development pipeline.

Can you assist with property development projects?

Absolutely. We provide end-to-end development services including planning, design, construction oversight, and strategic market analysis.

Can you implement identity and access management for our team?

Absolutely. We deploy role-based access controls, least-privilege policies, and strong authentication safeguards for critical systems.

Can you manage properties for diasporans?

Definitely! We can help you acquire, develop and manage properties for optimum returns no matter where you are in the world. We assist our international clients while giving them real-time updates with virtual tours and digital documentation.

Do you offer continuous monitoring and incident response?

Yes. We provide real-time threat monitoring, rapid alerts, and incident response actions to contain risks quickly and reduce downtime.

Do you offer investment advice for real estate?

Yes, our consultancy helps investors identify opportunities, assess risks, and develop strategies to maximize returns on real estate investments.

Can you train our staff on cybersecurity awareness?

Yes. Our practical training equips teams to identify phishing attempts, apply secure habits, and respond correctly to common cyber threats.
